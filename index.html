Game Rules

  1. Each player takes turns placing numbers 1-9 on the board
  2. After all numbers are placed, players take turns placing X and O symbols
  3. The first symbol must be placed on the cell containing number 1
  4. Win by getting three symbols in a row, column, or diagonal
  5. If no one wins, the player with the highest sum of their numbers wins
